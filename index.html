The Sustainable Design Impact Hub in New York

17 September 2026 | Newlab

Read More

With an emphasis on the intersection of Fashion & Deep Tech, the Sustainable Design Impact Hub in New York bridges the gap between lab and market while offering fresh insight into New York's fashion and innovation ecosystem.

We believe New York City is not only a runway, but a hub for deep-tech fashion innovation. Join us as we convene designers, scientists, entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders to shape the future of sustainable fashion.

Fashion x Deep Tech

Innovation Gallery

A curated presentation of design-forward deep tech built in New York.

The gallery will spotlight emerging companies, materials, products, and technologies shaping the future of fashion, manufacturing, mobility, and the built environment. Designed as an elevated exhibition rather than a traditional trade show, the space will bring founders and their work into direct conversation with investors, brands, designers, researchers, and industry leaders.

We are especially interested in New York-based innovations that demonstrate strong design thinking, technical ambition, and the potential to create meaningful environmental or social impact.
